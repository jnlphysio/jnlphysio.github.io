SERVICES

We provide physiotherapy and hydrotherapy services for all ages, tailored to your specific needs in the comfort of your home or preferred environment.

Adults

  • Pain management
  • Injury recovery and prevention
  • Managing chronic conditions such as Stroke, MS, Parkinson's disease, Huntington's disease and other Neurological or Genetic conditions affecting physical health
  • Equipment prescription

Kids & Young Adults

  • Improving gross motor skills such as balance, strength, coordination to help them with running, climbing, jumping
  • Improving fine motor skills developing hand-eye coordination and dexterity
  • Assistance with Neurological and Genetic conditions affecting motor development
  • Toe walking
  • Toileting and Continence
  • Equipment prescription

Babies

  • Supporting motor development and milestone achievement including tummy time, rolling, sitting, crawling, and walking
  • Treatment for conditions such as torticollis and other muscle and joint-related conditions
  • Assistance with Neurological and Genetic conditions affecting motor development

Hydrotherapy

  • Hydrotherapy services for children and adults
  • Improves mobility, strength and coordination
  • Supports motor development in a playful way
  • Low-impact rehabilitation after injury
  • Helps to improve or maintain fitness
  • Helps with chronic conditions affecting the body and tension related pain

About Us

With over 7 years in private practice, we've evolved our approach to physiotherapy. Rather than encouraging dependency through frequent treatments, we empower our clients to take an active role in their recovery.

Our focus is on guiding individuals to better understand their condition and the purpose of each intervention, ensuring every interaction is meaningful and contributes to long-term results.

We believe that personalised care and active participation are essential for successful outcomes. For some, this means providing information, hands-on treatment, and follow-up visits, while others may benefit from more regular sessions with additional guidance. This tailored approach ensures that clients are never over-treated or left to navigate recovery alone.
