About Us

With over 7 years in private practice, we've evolved our approach to physiotherapy. Rather than encouraging dependency through frequent treatments, we empower our clients to take an active role in their recovery.

Our focus is on guiding individuals to better understand their condition and the purpose of each intervention, ensuring every interaction is meaningful and contributes to long-term results.

We believe that personalized care and active participation are essential for successful outcomes. For some, this means providing information, hands-on treatment, and follow-up visits, while others may benefit from more regular sessions with additional guidance. This tailored approach ensures that clients are never over-treated or left to navigate recovery alone.

SERVICES

We provide personalized physiotherapy and hydrotherapy services for all ages, tailored to your specific needs in the comfort of your home or preferred environment.

Adults

  • Pain management
  • Injury recovery and prevention
  • Managing chronic conditions such as Stroke, MS, Parkinson's disease, Huntington's disease and other Neurological or Genetic conditions affecting physical health
  • Equipment prescription

Kids & Young Adults

  • Improving Gross motor skills such as balance, strength, coordination to help them with running, climbing, jumping
  • Improving Fine motor skills developing hand-eye coordination and dexterity
  • Assistance with Neurological and Genetic conditions affecting motor development
  • Toe walking
  • Toileting and Continence
  • Equipment prescription

Babies

  • Support motor milestones such as tummy time, rolling, sitting, crawling, and walking
  • Treatment for conditions such as club foot, torticollis, and other muscle and joint-related conditions
  • Assistance with Neurological and Genetic conditions affecting motor development

Reformer Pilates

  • 1:1 session with our physiotherapist in Murray Bridge
  • Helps recovering from injuries and surgery
  • Improves core muscle strength and postural control
  • Improves balance, overall body strength and control, and body awareness
  • Helps with chronic conditions affecting the body and tension related pain
